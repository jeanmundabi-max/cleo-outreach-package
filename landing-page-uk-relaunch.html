Questions
Everything you want to know before downloading
Is this the same Cleo as the one in the US?+
Yes. Same company. Same AI. Cleo was actually born in London in 2016. They went to the US to grow, hit over 4 million users, and now they're back home. The UK version focuses on AI coaching and budgeting. No cash advances in the UK (different regulations).
Why did Cleo leave the UK in the first place?+
Cleo focused on the US market where cash advances were the core product. The US market was bigger and faster growing for that product. Now Cleo is back with a different focus: AI money coaching. The CEO said "coming back feels like a homecoming."
Is my data safe?+
Yes. Cleo uses bank level encryption (256 bit). It is FCA registered. It has read only access to your bank account. It cannot move or spend your money. Your data is never sold. You can delete everything at any time.
Do I need to switch banks?+
No. Cleo connects to your existing bank account. Monzo, Starling, Revolut, Barclays, HSBC, Lloyds, NatWest. Whatever you use. Keep your bank. Add Cleo on top. It is a coaching layer, not a replacement.
How much does it cost?+
The free tier gives you AI chat, spending insights, and basic budgeting. Paid tiers unlock advanced features like savings automation, personalised coaching plans, and spending challenges. Paid plans start at GBP 5.99 per month. No commitment. Cancel anytime. No tricks.
What about the FTC thing I saw in the news?+
The FTC settlement in March 2025 was about cash advance advertising in the US. The UK version of Cleo does not offer cash advances. UK Cleo focuses on AI coaching, budgeting, and savings. Different product. Different regulations. FCA registered.
I already have Monzo. Why do I need Cleo too?+
Monzo shows you categories and transactions. Cleo tells you what to do about them. "You spent 28% of your income on eating out. Here is how to cut it to 15% without hating your life." That is the difference between a bank and a coach.
